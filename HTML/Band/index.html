About the band

Polyphia is a primarily instrumental progressive rock band based in Plano, Texas in 2019.
The group consists of guitarists Tim Henson and Scott LePage, bassist Clay Gober, and drummer Clay Aeschliman.
Polyphia's sound is noted for incorporating virtuosic guitar parts with other styles of music.
